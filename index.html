Day 1

Leaving the Plains Behind

On the Move

The journey begins on the North East Express out of Anand Vihar Terminal (ANVT). It’s a long haul, but watching the North Indian landscapes transition into the lush northeast through the train window is half the magic.

Resting Your Head

Once in Guwahati, take it easy. Crashing with friends or relatives saves the budget. If you have the energy, the bustling Fancy Bazaar or a quiet moment by the Brahmaputra at Kamakhya Temple are great ways to stretch your legs.

Curated Highlights

Places to Experience

01 · Shillong

The Eastern Highlands

  • Wander around Elephant Falls
  • Catch the view from Shillong Peak
  • Walk the Mawphlang Sacred Forest
  • Coffee and culture at Don Bosco Museum
02 · Cherrapunji (Sohra)

Where the Clouds Rest

  • Stand before the towering Nohkalikai Falls
  • Trek down to the Double Decker Root Bridge
  • Explore the depths of Mawsmai Cave
  • Find quiet at Wei Sawdong
03 · Dawki & Mawlynnong

The Glass River

  • Boat across the crystal-clear Umngot River
  • Perfect natural lighting for riverside self-portraits
  • Stroll through Asia's Cleanest Village
  • Look out over the Bangladesh plains
04 · Jowai & Jaintia Hills

Hidden Blue Pools

  • Swim in the surreal blue of Krang Suri Falls
  • Walk among the ancient Nartiang Monoliths
  • Take in the sweeping panoramas of the Jaintia Hills
05 · Guwahati

The Assam Gateway

  • Watch the sunset over the massive Brahmaputra
  • Hunt for souvenirs in Fancy Bazaar
  • Take a quick ferry to Umananda Island
